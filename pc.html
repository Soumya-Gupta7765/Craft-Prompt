The Lore of NeoCity

Syndicates and Shadows

Beneath the corporate towers, NeoCity's gangs rule like nations: the Circuitphage Hackers, the Razor Bruisers, the Night Runners, and the Violet Kings. Their members wear glitches as badges—your reputation is nothing without scars and exploits. Alignment can shift with a single hack or betrayal.

Ancient Rituals, Modern Tech

Initiation means proving your loyalty. Sometimes it's running mind-bending code through the city's mainframe, sometimes it's defending turf in back-alley brawls. Old rites meet new tech: encrypted tattoos, loyalty chips in your cortex, rites livestreamed for the city to judge.

Survival Protocols

A recruit who breaks protocol faces exile into the neon wastes. Encryption masks your trace, whispers protect you from rivals, and only allies can see your true face in the data fog. Legendary hackers once turned the entire police grid into static; one runner vanished from city cams for five years.

Ascension Pathways

For a select few, reputation rises to kingpin status. Urban legends whisper of the "Neon Oracle"—an AI said to guide the best. Syndicates only respect those who master secrecy, survive betrayal, and break the system from inside.
